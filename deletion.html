User Data Deletion Information

1. Right to Request Deletion

Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal information.

2. How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal information, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to support@aermedia.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  • Include your account information (e.g., email, username) for verification.

3. Verification Process

We will verify your identity before processing your request to ensure you are authorized to delete the data.

4. Processing Timeframe

We aim to process deletion requests within [insert time frame, e.g., 30 days] and will notify you once the deletion has been completed.

5. Exceptions to Deletion

We may retain certain personal information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Data necessary for fraud prevention or resolving disputes.

6. Impact of Deletion

Please note that deleting your personal information may result in the loss of access to certain features of our service.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this process, please reach out to us at support@aermedia.com.
